About us

Cafe & tea was registered and started by mk in Medavakkam in 2016. The first store was launched in annanagar,Chennai, in October 2016. Subsequently two other outlets opened within a fortnight’s time. At times, your simplest needs happen to be sadly out of your reach. You’ve always wanted to have a refreshing cup of coffee &tea on the go, or while at work, or get it delivered when you need it most. But invariably, you end up making compromises. You resort to a not-so-hygienic tea shop on the road, or spend your valuable time painfully dipping those tea bags into your cup. Being chronic coffee buffs ourselves, we genuinely wanted to put a stop to all those compromises. And we wanted to delight every coffee & tea lover with the pleasure of enjoying this lovely beverage. So we thought the best way to do this is to blend high quality Tea & COffee with top class service and give a wholesome experience to chai lovers.
